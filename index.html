About

This is an Anime Quiz Game Thingy I made.

In it you are shown a random show and three random directors and you're supposed to guess who the director of the show is.

You either type in your AniList-Account name or the number of the most popular shows you would like to be part of the selection of shows.

Then you'll get to select how many of em to use, so if you want it all to be really random, add a lot of entries in the first menu and then make the final selection small.

I recommend playing this on your smartphone btw.

Have fun.
